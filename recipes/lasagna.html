Lasagna Recipe

Delicious Lasagna

Classic Lasagna! This recipe will leave your mouth watering and your stomach grumbling! Enjoy as a weekend dinner or a midweek treat! The choice is yours.

Ingredients

  • 450g lean beef mince
  • 1 finely chopped onion
  • 1 finely chopped red pepper and the same with a green one
  • 150g sliced button mushrooms
  • 2 x 400g tins chopped tomatoes
  • 200g tomato puree
  • Salt and pepper to season
  • 1 tsp each of dried thyme, parsley, oregano
  • 2 bay leaves
  • 300g cottage cheese
  • 150g grated mature cheddar
  • 300g pasta sheets, dried or fresh
  • Oil for frying

Steps

  1. Brown the mince in the oil, then set aside.
  2. Now gently fry the onion, peppers and mushrooms until soft
  3. Pop the mince back in together with the tomato puree and bay leaves. Cook for 5 minutes.
  4. Now add the tomatoes and herbs, bring to the boil, then simmer for at least 30 minutes and season well
  5. Grease a 10” x 8” baking dish and line the bottom with pasta.
  6. Fish out the bay leaves and spoon about 1/3 of the mix on to the pasta, then put 'blobs' of cottage cheese (again about 1/3) on top of the mince. Carry on for 2 more layers, finishing with meat, cottage cheese and then cheddar.
  7. Bake at 180C for about 45 minutes to 1 hour.serve with salad and garlic bread.
